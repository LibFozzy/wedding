Frequently Asked Questions

Can I come to the elopement?

We’re not planning on inviting anyone to our elopement. It’s in another country, it’ll be expensive to attend, you won’t actually be able to see us get married.

We will be inviting people to a post-elopement celebration in Leith in November 2026

So what about the party?

We'll be sending out invites to the party in Leith in spring 2026. We've booked Leith Arches as the venue.

Is there a registry?

We’re not doing a registry, we’ll have been together for 14 years by the time we get married. We don’t need more stuff (we really don’t need more stuff).

If you do want to give a gift, you’re welcome to contribute to our mini-moon in Italy or make a donation to Cats Protection.

How does the ceremony work?

We won’t know exactly when we’re getting married until a week or two before we go. We’re hoping for a Wednesday lunchtime, but we’ll see what we get!

We’re allowed to take a witness in with us. It’ll take an hour or so to complete the process. We won’t be live-streaming it.
